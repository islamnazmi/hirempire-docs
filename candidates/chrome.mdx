### Understanding AI Results

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Match Score Breakdown">
**Score Ranges:**
- **90-100**: Exceptional match - immediate priority candidate
- **80-89**: Strong match - definitely worth pursuing  
- **70-79**: Good potential - consider based on other factors
- **60-69**: Moderate fit - may work for specific situations
- **Below 60**: Poor match - likely not worth pursuing

**Score Components:**
- Skills alignment (30%)
- Experience relevance (25%) 
- Career progression (20%)
- Cultural fit indicators (15%)
- Market availability (10%)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="AI Insights Categories">

- **Skills Analysis:** Detailed breakdown of technical and soft skills match

</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

**Common Issues:**
- Check that you have remaining AI evaluation credits
