## Step-by-Step Guide

### Installation Process

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Step 1: Install the Extension">
1. Click the [Chrome Web Store link](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/hirempire-headhunting/micnjpbgakkncginjpmbkdkkbbhomlmd)
2. Click "Add to Chrome" button
3. Confirm by clicking "Add Extension"
4. The Hirempire icon will appear in your browser toolbar
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 2: Connect Your Account">
1. Click the Hirempire extension icon
2. Enter your Hirempire login credentials
3. Grant necessary permissions
4. You'll see a "Connected" confirmation message
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 3: Start Adding Candidates">
1. Navigate to any LinkedIn profile
2. Click the Hirempire extension icon
3. Select the job from the dropdown menu
4. Click "Add to Job" - candidate is instantly saved
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Using the Extension

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Adding Candidates to Jobs">
**From LinkedIn Profile:**
1. Visit any LinkedIn candidate profile
2. Click the Hirempire extension icon in your toolbar
3. Choose the job position from the dropdown
4. Click "Add Candidate" - they're instantly added to your pipeline

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Managing Your Pipeline">
**Real-time Sync:**
- All added candidates appear instantly in your Hirempire dashboard
- Changes made in Hirempire reflect in the extension
- Cross-device synchronization for team collaboration

**Candidate Status:**
- See if a candidate is already in your system
- View which jobs they're associated with
- Quick access to candidate details and history
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Extension Not Working?">
**Common Solutions:**
- Refresh your LinkedIn page and try again
- Check that you're logged into your Hirempire account
- Ensure the extension has necessary permissions
- Try disabling and re-enabling the extension

**Still Having Issues?**
Contact our support team at [support@hirempire.com](mailto:support@hirempire.com) with your browser version and error details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="LinkedIn Compliance">
**Safe & Compliant:**
- Our extension follows all LinkedIn terms of service
- We only capture publicly available profile information
- No automated scraping or bulk data extraction
- Respects LinkedIn's rate limits and guidelines

**Data Usage:**

</AccordionGroup>
