## What is Hirempire?

Hirempire is a complete recruitment platform built by recruiters, for recruiters. It brings together everything your team needs to hire — job posting, candidate management, AI screening, team collaboration, scheduling, and analytics — in one place.

Whether you're a solo recruiter or a growing HR team, Hirempire scales with you.
