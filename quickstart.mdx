---

## 1. Create your account

Go to [app.hirempire.com](https://app.hirempire.com) and sign up. After confirming your email you'll land in your workspace dashboard.

<Tip>
  If you were invited by a teammate, use the invite link from your email — it will automatically join you to the right workspace.
</Tip>

## 2. Set up your company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Companies in the sidebar">
    Click **Companies** in the left sidebar to open the companies page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Company'">
    Fill in your company name, upload a logo, select your industry and size, and optionally add your website, LinkedIn URL, and location. This information appears on your public job listings and application pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your culture page (optional)">
    From the company card, click **Culture** to add a culture description, team photos, and testimonials. This appears on your public career page to attract candidates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 3. Post your first job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Jobs' in the sidebar">
    Then click **New Job** in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the AI Job Wizard">
    Hirempire's AI wizard guides you through the job creation process conversationally — it asks for the role, department, location, salary, and generates a full job description automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add application sources">
    Enter the platforms where you'll share the job (e.g., LinkedIn, Indeed). Each source gets a unique tracking URL so you can measure where your best candidates come from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the job to Active">
    Only active jobs accept applications. Toggle the status when you're ready to go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 4. Share the application link

After saving, click **Application URLs** on the job card to copy source-specific links. Share them on your chosen platforms.

<Info>
  Each source URL is unique — candidates who apply through different links are automatically tagged by source so you can track performance.
</Info>

## 5. Manage incoming candidates

Applications appear under **Talents** in the sidebar. You'll see a Kanban board with columns for each stage of your pipeline (New, Reviewing, Interview, Offer, Hired, Rejected).

- Drag cards between stages to update status
- Click any candidate to view their CV, answers, and AI screening score
- Use the **CV Bank** to store candidates who aren't right for the current role but might be great later

## 6. Invite your team (optional)

Go to **Settings → Members** to invite colleagues. Assign roles:

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| Owner | Full access, billing |
| Admin | Full access except billing |
| Member | Jobs, candidates, collaboration |
| Department Manager | Requisitions, meetings, chat, tasks |
