## Setup Your Recruitment Process

Follow these simple steps to start managing your hiring process with Hirempire.

### Step 1: Create Your Company Profile

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Add Your First Company">
    Navigate to [Companies](https://hirempire.com/companies) and click "Add a company"
    
    **Required Information:**
    - Company logo (mandatory for recognition)
    - Company name
    - Company domain (this links your jobs and creates branded application pages)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="user-tie" title="Set Up Company Details">
    For employers, you can edit your company profile from the home page under "My Company"
    
    - Update company name
    - Change company logo
    - Manage company status (Active/Inactive)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Post Your First Job

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="briefcase" title="Create a Job Posting">
    Go to [Post a new job](https://hirempire.com/choose-the-company) and fill in:
    
    1. **Position title** - Be clear and specific
    2. **Department** - Choose from available options
    3. **Location** - Country, region, or "Worldwide"
    4. **Salary** - Exact amount, range, or leave confidential
    5. **Job type** - Remote, Hybrid, or On-site
    6. **Job description** - Will be auto-formatted with headers
    7. **Source platforms** - Comma-separated list for tracking
    8. **Special questions** - Optional additional screening questions
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="link" title="Get Application URLs">
    After creating your job, you'll receive trackable URLs for each platform you specified
    
    - Share different URLs on different platforms
    - Track which sources bring the best candidates
    - Monitor application sources in real-time
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Set Up Automation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="Enable Auto Follow-ups">
    Navigate to [Auto Follow-ups](https://hirempire.com/automated-follow-up)
    
    1. Add your calendar link (e.g., Calendly URL)
    2. Click "Activate"
    3. Candidates will automatically receive:
       - Acceptance emails with interview booking links
       - Professional rejection emails
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="robot" title="Activate AI Screening (Paid Plans)">
    Available with Starter, Pro, and Premium plans
    
    - Candidates are automatically screened upon application
    - Get instant insights without manual CV review
    - Access via [AI Screening](https://hirempire.com/ai-screening)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pro Tips for Success

<Tip>
  **Job Posting Best Practice**: Use specific job titles and comprehensive descriptions. The clearer your posting, the better quality candidates you'll attract.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Source Tracking**: Always specify your posting sources (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.) to track which platforms work best for your roles.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Status Management**: Only active jobs accept applications. Remember to activate/deactivate jobs based on your hiring needs.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  **Plan Limits**: Keep track of your active job limits based on your subscription plan. You can have unlimited total jobs, but only a certain number can be active at once.
</Warning>
