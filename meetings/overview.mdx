## Overview

The Meetings page shows all meetings booked through your scheduling page — interviews, discovery calls, follow-ups — in one place. It syncs with your connected calendar so you always have an up-to-date view of what's coming up.

## Connecting your calendar

Hirempire integrates with **Cal.com** for scheduling. To connect:

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations**
2. Click **Connect Cal.com**
3. Follow the OAuth flow to authorize access

Once connected, all meetings booked through your Cal.com scheduling page will appear in Hirempire automatically.

<Info>
  Department managers do not see the calendar connection banner — meetings are visible to all team members once the workspace calendar is connected.
</Info>

## Calendar views

Switch between **Calendar** and **List** views using the toggle in the top right:

- **Calendar view** — monthly, weekly, or daily grid with meetings plotted in time slots
- **List view** — chronological list of all meetings, filterable by status

## Meeting card

Click any meeting to open its detail card, which shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|------------|
| **Attendee** | Candidate name and email |
| **Date & Time** | Meeting time in the attendee's timezone |
| **Duration** | Length of the meeting in minutes |
| **Calendar** | The Cal.com username the meeting was booked on |
| **Status** | Upcoming or Completed |

## Joining a meeting

If the meeting has a video link (e.g., Google Meet, Zoom), a **Join Meeting** button appears in the detail card. Click it to open the call directly.

## Inviting team members

To invite a colleague to a specific meeting:

1. Click the meeting to open its detail card
2. Click **Invite** in the footer
3. Select from your team members list or enter an email address directly
4. Click **Invite** — they'll receive a meeting invitation email

## Upcoming count

The header shows a badge with the number of upcoming meetings so you can see your load at a glance.
