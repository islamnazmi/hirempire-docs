## What is Interview Intelligence?

Interview Intelligence automates everything that happens around your interviews — recording, transcription, evaluation, and reporting. Run the meeting normally; Hirempire captures the conversation in the background and produces a structured report when it's done.

It replaces manual note-taking and brings consistency to multi-interviewer panels, so your team can make faster, less biased hiring decisions.

**What it delivers:**
- 100% of interviews captured automatically
- Faster candidate reviews with scored, searchable transcripts
- Zero manual notes required

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run the interview">
    Conduct the interview as you normally would in your video tool (Google Meet, Zoom, etc.). Hirempire's AI bot joins the call and records in the background — no extra setup needed during the meeting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI processes the recording">
    After the call ends, Hirempire transcribes the audio, maps each response to the job's requirements, and analyzes the candidate's performance. Processing typically takes a few minutes — the meeting card shows a processing indicator while it runs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review insights instantly">
    Once done, a **View Interview Intelligence** button appears on the meeting detail card. You'll have a full scorecard, transcript, and highlights ready before your next meeting — and one-click sharing for the team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's in the report

<Frame>
  ![Interview Intelligence report](/images/interview-intelligence.png)
</Frame>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="star" title="Overall Score">
    A 0–100 score representing the candidate's overall performance in the interview. Calculated from all evaluated competencies.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="list-check" title="Structured Scorecard">
    A multi-competency breakdown — communication, technical skills, problem solving, culture fit, and more. Each competency gets an individual score and brief notes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="thumbs-up" title="AI Decision">
    A categorical recommendation: **Accept**, **Hold**, or **Reject** — along with the plain-language reasoning behind it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="file-lines" title="Automatic Transcription">
    A full, searchable transcript of the entire interview, with both interviewer and candidate dialogue clearly attributed. Available the moment processing completes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="comments-question" title="Question & Answer Analysis">
    Each question and the candidate's response is reviewed individually. The AI surfaces red flags — vague answers, inconsistencies, or concerning responses — so you can jump straight to the moments that matter.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="clapperboard" title="Time-Stamped Highlights">
    Standout moments — strong answers, red flags, key examples — are detected automatically and time-stamped. Highlights are shareable as video clips so you can send a teammate the exact moment instead of the whole recording.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="bolt" title="Instant Summary">
    An executive overview of the interview, ready for one-click team distribution. Useful for hiring panels and async stakeholder updates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Credibility Signals">
    Indicators the AI detected about the candidate's authenticity — confidence level, consistency of answers, and any flags worth noting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Engagement Timeline">
    A timeline view of how engaged the candidate was throughout the interview — energy levels, response depth, and participation patterns.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="person" title="Interviewer Analysis">
    Feedback on the interview itself — question quality, time balance, follow-up depth. Helps interviewers improve over time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="flag" title="Compliance Flags">
    Highlights any potentially problematic questions or topics raised during the interview to help maintain fair and compliant hiring practices.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="message-smile" title="Candidate Feedback">
    A summary of the candidate's own signals about their interest level and how they perceived the role and company.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Live transcript

During the call, Hirempire shows a live transcript with both sides of the conversation in real time. When the AI detects a standout response, an **AI highlight detected** indicator appears with quick context on why the moment was notable — useful for interviewers who want to follow up immediately.

## Candidate consent

<Warning>
  Candidates must consent to being recorded before Interview Intelligence can be used. Hirempire includes a consent prompt at the start of the recorded session. Do not use Interview Intelligence without obtaining explicit consent from the candidate.
</Warning>

Hirempire handles the consent flow automatically — the AI bot announces its presence at the start of the call and records only after the candidate acknowledges.

## Sharing reports

Every report is shareable with one click. Send the full report, or share an individual time-stamped highlight clip to a teammate — they'll see the exact moment without having to scrub through the recording.

## Accessing past reports

All Interview Intelligence reports are stored on the meeting record. Open any past meeting from the [Meetings page](/meetings/overview) and click **View Interview Intelligence** to re-read the analysis at any time.

## Status indicators

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Recording received, analysis not started |
| `recording` | Recording in progress |
| `processing` | AI is analyzing the recording |
| `done` | Report is ready |
| `failed` | Analysis failed — contact support |

## Best practices

<Tip>
  **Define competencies on the job posting.** The scorecard maps responses against the role's requirements — clearer requirements produce more useful scorecards.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Use highlights for hiring panels.** Instead of asking teammates to watch a 45-minute recording, share two or three time-stamped highlights. Faster decisions, same context.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Review compliance flags before rejecting.** If a flag appears, check the transcript moment before acting on the AI's recommendation.
</Tip>
