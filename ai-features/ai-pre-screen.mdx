## What is AI Pre-Screen?

AI Pre-Screen is an asynchronous, proctored interview that runs entirely on its own — no scheduling, no recruiter time, no extra tools. You send a candidate a one-time link, the AI runs a live structured interview with proctoring active, and you get a scored transcript and recommendation as soon as they finish.

It's how you replace the first-round phone screen with something faster, more consistent, and less biased.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send the link">
    From an applicant card, click **AI Pre-Screen**. A unique, one-time interview link is generated and emailed to the candidate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Candidate interviews">
    The candidate accepts the ground rules, then has a live conversation with the AI. Proctoring is active throughout — tab-switching, paste attempts, and other integrity violations are detected in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review results">
    You're notified the moment the interview completes (or terminates early). Open the candidate panel to see the score, scorecard, AI recommendation, and full transcript.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Proctoring & integrity

Candidates are told upfront that this is a proctored evaluation. The session enforces several anti-cheating measures:

| Check | What it does |
|-------|------------|
| **Tab-switching detection** | Session terminates instantly if the candidate navigates away from the interview page. You're notified immediately. |
| **Paste blocking** | Copy-paste is disabled at the input level — answers must be typed. |
| **Real-time typing** | The AI watches for natural typing patterns rather than pasted blocks of text. |
| **Ground rules enforcement** | Candidates must explicitly accept the rules before the interview starts. |

The AI also probes for specifics — vague or generic answers get followed up on, so candidates can't coast on rehearsed responses.

## When to use it

AI Pre-Screen replaces the first-round phone screen. Use it when:

- You have **high applicant volume** and need to triage faster
- You want **consistent evaluation** across every applicant for a role
- You want to **reduce bias** in the first-round filter
- Your candidates are **distributed across time zones** and scheduling is painful

## Sending a Pre-Screen

1. Open the candidate from the **Talents** page or a job's applicants list
2. Click **AI Pre-Screen** on the applicant card
3. The candidate receives an email with a unique link
4. Track status from the candidate panel — Sent, In Progress, Completed, or Terminated

## Best practices

<Tip>
  **Use it as the first filter, not the only one.** AI Pre-Screen is designed to replace the initial phone screen — a hiring decision should still combine pre-screen results with CV analysis and a human interview.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Tell candidates what to expect.** A short note in your application confirmation email explaining that they'll receive a proctored AI interview reduces drop-off and integrity violations.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Check terminated sessions before rejecting.** A tab switch can sometimes be accidental. Review the transcript before making a decision.
</Tip>
