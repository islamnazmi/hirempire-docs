Once you've [connected the MCP Server](/mcp/quickstart), Claude has access to the following tools.

## Jobs

| Tool | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| `list_jobs` | Get all jobs in your workspace |
| `create_job` | Create a new job posting |

## Applicants

| Tool | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| `get_applicants` | Fetch candidates for a specific job |
| `update_applicant_status` | Move a candidate to a new pipeline stage |

## Companies

| Tool | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| `get_companies` | List your workspace companies |

## Example prompts

Each tool can be invoked with natural language — Claude picks the right tool based on what you ask:

- *"Show me all jobs in my workspace"* → `list_jobs`
- *"Create a Senior Backend Engineer job in the Engineering department"* → `create_job`
- *"Who applied to the Marketing Manager role?"* → `get_applicants`
- *"Move Ahmed Hassan to the Interview stage"* → `update_applicant_status`
- *"Which companies do I have set up?"* → `get_companies`
