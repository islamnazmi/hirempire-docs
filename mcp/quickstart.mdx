Follow these steps to connect Claude AI to your Hirempire workspace via the MCP Server.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Claude AI (MCP)">
    In Hirempire, open **Settings** and click **Claude AI (MCP)** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Connect to Claude'">
    This opens [claude.ai/settings/connectors](https://claude.ai/settings/connectors) in a new tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a custom connector">
    Click **Add custom connector** and fill in the details shown on the settings page:

    | Field | Value |
    |-------|-------|
    | Name | `Hirempire` |
    | Remote MCP server URL | `https://api.hirempire.com/v1/mcp` |
    | OAuth Client ID | Copy from [Settings → Claude AI (MCP)](https://app.hirempire.com/dashboard/settings/mcp) |
    | OAuth Client Secret | Copy from [Settings → Claude AI (MCP)](https://app.hirempire.com/dashboard/settings/mcp) |

    Use the copy buttons on the settings page for convenience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect and authorize">
    Click **Connect**. Claude will redirect you back to Hirempire to authorize access — make sure you're logged in to your Hirempire account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You must be logged in to Hirempire when Claude redirects back for authorization. The OAuth flow connects Claude specifically to your workspace.
</Info>
