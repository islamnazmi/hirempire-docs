## Company Management Overview

Navigate to [Companies](https://hirempire.com/companies) to view and manage all your company profiles. Each company can have its own jobs, branding, and settings.

## Adding a New Company

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Navigate to Companies**: Go to your companies dashboard
2. **Click "Add a company"**: Start the creation process
3. **Fill in Required Information**:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="image" title="Upload Company Logo">
    **Required Step**: Upload the company's logo
    - This is mandatory for company recognition
    - Used on job postings and application pages
    - Supports standard image formats (PNG, JPG, SVG)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Enter Company Name">
    **Company Name**: Add the official company name
    - This appears on all job postings
    - Used in candidate communications
    - Can be edited later if needed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Add Company Domain">
    **Domain Setup**: Enter the company website domain
    - Links your jobs to the company
    - Creates branded application pages
    - Format: `example.com` (without http://)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Managing Existing Companies

### Editing Company Information

Click the **"Edit"** button on any company to modify:

- **Company Status**: Toggle between Active/Inactive
  - Inactive status is for organization only
  - Does not affect published jobs
  - Helps you organize multiple companies

- **Company Details**: Update name, logo, or domain
- **Branding Elements**: Modify visual appearance

### Company Status Management

<Info>
  Company status (Active/Inactive) is purely organizational and doesn't affect job functionality. It's designed to help you organize multiple companies in your dashboard.
</Info>

## Deleting Companies

<Warning>
  Deleting a company will remove all associated data. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

### Deletion Process

1. **Locate Company**: Find the company you want to delete
2. **Click Three Dots Menu**: Access additional options
3. **Select "Delete"**: Confirm the deletion

**What Gets Deleted**:
- Company profile and branding
- Associated job postings
- Application tracking for that company
- Candidate data linked to company jobs

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Logo Upload Problems">
    **Supported Formats**: PNG, JPG, JPEG, SVG
    **Size Limits**: Maximum file size varies by plan
    **Solution**: Ensure your image meets format and size requirements
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Domain Not Working">
    **Check Format**: Use `example.com` format (no http:// or https://)
    **Verify Domain**: Ensure the domain is active and accessible
    **DNS Propagation**: Changes may take time to reflect
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cannot Delete Company">
    **Active Jobs**: Deactivate or delete all associated jobs first
    **Pending Applications**: Process or archive candidate applications
    **Contact Support**: Reach out if issues persist
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
