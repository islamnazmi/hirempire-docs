## Calendar Integration Overview

Hirempire allows applicants to **book interviews directly on your calendar** after they pass screening stages.

By connecting your **Cal.com** account, you enable:

- Automatic interview booking links in candidate emails
- Real-time availability from your calendar
- Seamless scheduling without back-and-forth emails
- A professional booking experience for applicants

---

## Integration Flow


Follow these simple steps to connect Cal.com with Hirempire.

### Step 1 — Connect Your Cal.com Account

1. Go to: [My Calendar](https://hirempire.com/my-calendar)
2. Click **Connect Cal.com**
3. You will be redirected to Cal.com to authenticate
4. Approve access for Hirempire

After successful authentication, you will be redirected back to Hirempire.

---

### Step 2 — Choose the Event Type

Once you return to Hirempire:

- You will see all your Cal.com event types (e.g., 15-min call, 30-min interview, HR screening)
- Select the event you want applicants to book
- This event will be used in all interview booking emails

---

### Step 3 — Choose Your Hirempire Scheduling Username

You will be prompted to choose a **Hirempire scheduling username**.

This creates your public scheduling link inside Hirempire, such as:

`https://hirempire.com/meet?id=your-username`

Applicants will use this link (connected to your Cal.com event) to book interviews.

<Info>
  **Availability**: Hirempire allows candidates to book time slots up to **one week in advance** only.
</Info>


---

## Managing Your Calendar Integration

### Changing Your Event Type

If you want applicants to book a different event:

1. Go to https://hirempire.com/my-calendar
2. Disconnect your calendar
3. Click **Connect Cal.com**
4. Authenticate again

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  Make sure your selected Cal.com event has enough availability slots for interviews.
</Tip>

<Tip>

</Tip>

<Tip>
  Test your scheduling link yourself after setup to ensure everything works correctly.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cal.com Not Connecting">
    **Possible Causes**:
    - You denied permissions during authentication
    - Your Cal.com session expired
    - Browser popup blocker prevented redirect

    **Solutions**:
    - Retry the connection process
    - Ensure popups are allowed
    - Log into Cal.com first, then retry
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Event Types Not Showing">
    **Possible Causes**:
    - No events created in Cal.com
    - Events set to private
    - Wrong Cal.com account connected

    **Solutions**:
    - Create an event in Cal.com first
    - Ensure the event is active and public
    - Reconnect the correct account
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Applicants Cannot Book Time">
    **Possible Causes**:
    - No availability in your calendar
    - Event duration too long
    - Calendar conflict rules in Cal.com

    **Solutions**:
    - Increase availability in Cal.com
    - Shorten event duration if needed
    - Review your availability settings
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
