## Automated Follow-ups Overview

Never miss a candidate follow-up again! Hirempire's automated email system handles professional candidate communication based on status changes, ensuring consistent and timely responses throughout your hiring process.

## How Automated Follow-ups Work

### Trigger-Based Communication

**Automatic Email Sending**:
- **Status-Based Triggers**: Emails sent when candidate status changes
- **Professional Templates**: Pre-written, branded email templates
- **Instant Delivery**: Emails sent immediately upon status updates
- **Consistent Experience**: Same professional tone for all candidates

### Email Types

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="badge-check" title="Acceptance Emails">
    **Sent When**: Candidate status changed to `Accepted`
    
    **Email Contents**:
    - Congratulations message
    - Next steps information
    - Company contact information
    - Professional, welcoming tone
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="shield-x" title="Rejection Emails">
    **Sent When**: Candidate status changed to "Rejected"
    
    **Email Contents**:
    - Professional, respectful rejection message
    - Thanks for their interest and time
    - Encouragement to apply for future roles
    - Maintains positive employer brand
    - Optional feedback inclusion
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Interview Scheduling">
    **Sent When**: Candidate status changed to "Interview Scheduled"
    
    **Email Contents**:
    - Interview confirmation
    - Calendar booking link
    - Interview preparation information
    - Contact details for questions
    - Meeting logistics
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Setting Up Automated Follow-ups

### Initial Setup Process

**Step-by-Step Activation**:

1. **Navigate to Follow-ups**: Go to [Auto Follow-ups](https://hirempire.com/automated-follow-up)
2. **Add Calendar Link**: Enter your scheduling URL (e.g., Calendly, Acuity, etc.)
3. **Click "Activate"**: Enable the automated system
4. **Confirmation**: Receive setup confirmation

### Calendar Integration Requirements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="calendar" title="Supported Calendar Platforms">
    **Compatible Services**:
    - **Calendly**: `https://calendly.com/yourname`
    - **Acuity Scheduling**: Your Acuity booking link
    - **Book Like a Boss**: Your BLAB scheduling URL
    - **SimplyBook.me**: Your booking page URL
    - **Any scheduling service**: That provides a direct booking link
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="link" title="Calendar Link Format">
    **Required Format**: Complete URL including `https://`
    
    **Examples**:
    - ✅ `https://calendly.com/john-smith`
    - ✅ `https://acuityscheduling.com/schedule.php?owner=12345`
    - ❌ `calendly.com/john-smith` (missing https://)
    - ❌ `john-smith` (incomplete URL)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Managing Automated Follow-ups

### Activation Status

**Current Status Display**:
- **Active**: Green indicator showing follow-ups are working
- **Inactive**: Red indicator showing follow-ups are disabled
- **Setup Required**: Yellow indicator showing calendar link needed

### Deactivating Follow-ups

**When to Deactivate**:
- Taking a break from hiring
- Switching to manual communication
- Calendar link no longer valid
- Testing different communication strategies

**Deactivation Process**:
1. Go to [Auto Follow-ups](https://hirempire.com/automated-follow-up)
2. Click "Deactivate"
3. Confirm deactivation
4. Follow-ups stop immediately

### Updating Calendar Information

**Changing Your Calendar Link**:
1. Navigate to [Profile Settings](https://hirempire.com/profile)
2. Update calendar booking URL
3. Save changes
4. New link applies to future follow-up emails

<Tip>
  **Pro Tip**: Test your calendar link regularly to ensure candidates can successfully book appointments.
</Tip>

## Email Template Examples

### Acceptance Email Sample

```
Subject: Congratulations! Next Steps for [Position Title] at [Company Name]

Dear [Candidate Name],

Congratulations! We're excited to move forward with your application for the [Position Title] role at [Company Name].

We were impressed with your qualifications and believe you'd be a great addition to our team.

Next Steps:
Please use the link below to schedule your next interview:
[Calendar Booking Link]

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us directly.

We look forward to speaking with you soon!

Best regards,
[Company Name] Hiring Team
```

### Rejection Email Sample

```
Subject: Thank you for your interest in [Position Title] at [Company Name]

Dear [Candidate Name],

Thank you for taking the time to apply for the [Position Title] position at [Company Name]. We appreciate your interest in joining our team.

After careful consideration, we have decided to move forward with other candidates whose experience more closely matches our current needs.

We were impressed with your background and encourage you to apply for future opportunities that align with your skills and experience.

Thank you again for your interest in [Company Name].

Best regards,
[Company Name] Hiring Team
```

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Emails Not Sending">
    **Common Causes**:
    - Follow-ups not activated
    - Invalid calendar link format
    - Candidate email address invalid
    - Status not properly updated
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Check activation status in Auto Follow-ups page
    - Verify calendar link format (must include https://)
    - Test with known good email address
    - Confirm status change was saved
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Calendar Link Not Working">
    **Possible Issues**:
    - Calendar link expired or changed
    - Scheduling service temporarily down
    - Calendar availability not set properly
    - Link format incorrect
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Test calendar link directly in browser
    - Update link in profile settings if changed
    - Check scheduling service status
    - Ensure proper URL format with https://
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Candidates Not Booking Interviews">
    **Potential Causes**:
    - No available time slots
    - Calendar link leads to error page
    - Email went to spam folder
    - Candidate changed mind about position
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Increase calendar availability
    - Test booking process yourself
    - Follow up personally with high-priority candidates
    - Check email deliverability
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
