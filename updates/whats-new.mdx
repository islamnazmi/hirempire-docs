<Update label="2025" description="Major Platform Expansion" tags={["New Feature", "Platform"]}>

Hirempire has been completely rebuilt and expanded with a full suite of team and recruiting tools. Here's what's new:

**Team Collaboration**
- **Real-time Chat** — team channels, direct messages, @mentions, emoji reactions, and typing indicators
- **Shared Tasks** — a team task board with statuses, assignments, and filters
- **Notification Inbox** — centralized feed for mentions, reactions, and channel invites

**Meetings & Interview Intelligence**
- **Meetings Page** — all scheduled interviews in Calendar and List views, with a full meeting detail card and Join Meeting button
- **Interview Intelligence** — AI analysis of recorded interviews: overall score, competency scorecard, hiring recommendation, engagement timeline, and compliance flags

**Recruiting Workflow**
- **Requisitions** — structured internal hiring requests for department managers to submit before a job goes live
- **AI Job Creation Wizard** — conversational AI that builds complete job postings through guided questions

**Analytics**
- **Analytics Dashboard** — source performance, pipeline funnel, time-to-hire, and application volume

**Workspace & Settings**
- **Role-based access control** — Owner, Admin, Member, and Department Manager roles with tailored page access
- **Culture Page Builder** — employer brand page with hero image, description, testimonials, and photo gallery

**Developer Tools**
- **MCP Server** — connect Claude AI to your Hirempire workspace for natural language recruiting actions
</Update>
