Stay up to date with the latest features, improvements, and enhancements to Hirempire.

<Update label="April 2026" description="MCP Server — Claude AI Integration" tags={["New Feature", "AI", "Developer"]}>

Connect Claude AI directly to your Hirempire workspace using the new **MCP Server** integration. Once connected, you can query candidates, manage jobs, and get AI-powered insights through natural language conversation — without leaving Claude.

**Example prompts:**
- "Show me all candidates for the Senior Developer role"
- "What's the status of Ahmed's application?"
- "Draft a rejection email for candidates in the Reviewing stage"

Go to **Settings → Claude AI (MCP)** to connect.

[Read the MCP Server guide](/ai-features/mcp-server)
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Interview Intelligence — AI Interview Analysis" tags={["New Feature", "AI", "Meetings"]}>

After a recorded interview ends, Hirempire's AI automatically analyzes it and produces a structured report — so your team can make faster, more consistent hiring decisions.

**Each report includes:**
- Overall candidate score (0–100)
- Competency scorecard (communication, technical skills, problem solving, culture fit)
- AI hiring recommendation: Strong Hire, Hire, No Hire, or Strong No Hire
- Engagement timeline showing candidate energy and participation
- Credibility signals and compliance flags
- Interviewer feedback to help your team improve over time

[Read the Interview Intelligence guide](/meetings/interview-intelligence)
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Meetings Page" tags={["New Feature", "Meetings"]}>

All your scheduled interviews and candidate meetings are now in one place. The Meetings page syncs with your connected Cal.com calendar and shows every booking in **Calendar** or **List** view.

**Key features:**
- Monthly, weekly, and daily calendar views
- Meeting detail card with attendee info, duration, and video link
- Join Meeting button for video calls
- Invite team members to any meeting directly from Hirempire

[Read the Meetings guide](/meetings/overview)
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Team Chat" tags={["New Feature", "Collaboration"]}>

Your recruiting team can now communicate in real time without switching tools. Hirempire Chat includes channels, direct messages, @mentions, emoji reactions, and typing indicators — all built into your hiring workspace.

**Highlights:**
- Public and private channels
- Mention candidates (`@@name`), jobs (`@@@name`), and tasks (`@@@@name`) as clickable links
- Rich text formatting with bold, italic, headings, and lists
- Reaction notifications in your Inbox

[Read the Chat guide](/collaboration/chat)
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Tasks — Shared Team Task Board" tags={["New Feature", "Collaboration"]}>

Manage recruiting action items as a team with the shared Tasks board. Create tasks, assign them to teammates, and track progress through four stages: **To Do → In Progress → In Review → Done**.

Filter by status, assignee, or search by title to find what you need fast.

[Read the Tasks guide](/collaboration/tasks)
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Inbox — Notification Center" tags={["New Feature", "Collaboration"]}>

The Inbox collects all notifications relevant to you — chat mentions, emoji reactions, channel invites, and workspace activity — in one feed. Stay on top of what needs your attention without leaving Hirempire.

[Read the Inbox guide](/collaboration/inbox)
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Requisitions — Internal Hiring Requests" tags={["New Feature", "Recruiting"]}>

Department managers can now submit structured internal hiring requests before a job goes live. HR reviews and approves requisitions, then converts them into actual job postings — creating a clear approval workflow between managers and recruiters.

**Requisition fields:** Job title, department, employment type, workplace type, location, priority (Low / Medium / High), target start date, and description.

Department Managers have access to Requisitions without needing full recruiter access.

[Read the Requisitions guide](/core-features/requisitions)
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="AI Job Creation Wizard" tags={["New Feature", "AI", "Jobs"]}>

Creating a job posting is now a conversation. The **AI Job Wizard** walks you through each detail step by step, then generates a complete, professionally written job description automatically.

**The wizard covers:**
- Job title, department, location, and workplace type
- Career level, employment type, and salary range
- Application sources and custom questions

You can edit the generated description before saving, or skip the wizard and use the standard form.

[Read the AI Job Wizard guide](/ai-features/job-wizard)
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Analytics Dashboard" tags={["New Feature", "Analytics"]}>

Get a data-driven view of your entire recruiting operation with the new Analytics dashboard.

**What's tracked:**
- Total applications, hired count, time-to-hire, and active jobs
- Source performance with conversion rates (hired ÷ applications per source)
- Pipeline funnel showing drop-off between stages
- Time-to-hire trend over time

Analytics is available on **Pro** and higher plans.

[Read the Analytics guide](/analytics/overview)
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Role-Based Access Control" tags={["New Feature", "Settings", "Team"]}>

Hirempire now has four distinct roles — **Owner**, **Admin**, **Member**, and **Department Manager** — each with tailored access to the right pages and features.

Department Managers get targeted access to Requisitions, Meetings, Chat, and Tasks without seeing the full recruiting pipeline.

[Read the Members & Roles guide](/settings/members-roles)
</Update>
