<Info>
  Tasks is available on **Pro** and higher plans.
</Info>

## Overview

The Tasks page gives your team a shared board to manage recruiting action items — from scheduling calls, to reviewing CVs, to following up with candidates. Everyone on the team can see what's been assigned and what's pending.

## Task Statuses

Tasks move through four stages:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **To Do** | Not started yet |
| **In Progress** | Actively being worked on |
| **In Review** | Needs a second look or approval |
| **Done** | Completed |

## Creating a Task

Click **New Task** and fill in:

- **Title** — what needs to be done
- **Description** — optional context or instructions
- **Assigned to** — pick a team member responsible for the task
- **Status** — set the initial state

## Assigning Tasks

Tasks can be assigned to any workspace member. The assigned person's avatar appears on the task card and they'll be notified.

<Tip>
  Use tasks to delegate action items that come out of chat conversations — keep decisions in chat, execution in Tasks.
</Tip>

## Filtering and searching

Use the search and filter controls at the top of the Tasks page to:

- Filter by status
- Filter by assigned team member
- Search by task title

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Keep tasks specific and actionable.** "Review Ahmed's CV for Senior Dev role" is better than "CV review."
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Use "In Review" as a handoff stage.** When you complete your part of a task but need someone else to sign off, move it to In Review rather than Done.
</Tip>
