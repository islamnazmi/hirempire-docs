</Info>

## Overview

The Inbox collects all notifications that are relevant to you — mentions in chat, reactions to your messages, channel invites, and other workspace activity. It's your single place to stay on top of what needs your attention without leaving Hirempire.

## Notification types

| Type | Triggered by |
|------|-------------|
| **Chat mention** | Someone @mentioned you in a channel or DM |
| **Reaction** | Someone reacted to one of your messages |
| **Channel invite** | You were added to a private channel |
| **General** | Workspace-level announcements or system notifications |

## Reading notifications

Open the Inbox from the sidebar. Unread notifications are shown with a red dot on the Inbox icon. Clicking a notification takes you directly to the relevant message or event.

## Unread badge

The sidebar shows a count of unread notifications on the Inbox icon. The count clears as you read notifications.

<Tip>
  Check your inbox at the start of each day to catch any mentions or action items from teammates in different time zones.
</Tip>
