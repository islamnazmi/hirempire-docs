## Cal.com (Calendar & Scheduling)

Hirempire integrates with **Cal.com** to bring your scheduled interviews and meetings into the platform.

### Connecting Cal.com

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations">
    Open Settings from the sidebar and click Integrations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Cal.com">
    Click the Connect button under the Cal.com section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize access">
    You'll be redirected to Cal.com to authorize Hirempire. Log in to your Cal.com account and approve the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    You're connected. New meetings booked through your Cal.com scheduling page will appear in Hirempire's Meetings page automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What syncs

Once connected, Hirempire pulls:
- All meetings booked via your Cal.com scheduling links
- Attendee name, email, and timezone
- Meeting time, duration, and video conferencing link
- Meeting status (upcoming / completed)

### Disconnecting

To disconnect Cal.com, click **Disconnect** in the Integrations settings. Existing meeting records are retained in Hirempire but no new meetings will sync.
