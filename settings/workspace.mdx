## Accessing Workspace Settings

Go to **Settings** in the sidebar, then select **Workspace** from the top menu.

<Info>
  Only **Owners** and **Admins** can edit workspace settings.
</Info>

## Workspace name and slug

The workspace name is the internal identifier for your team. The workspace slug is the unique identifier used in your workspace URL. Both can be updated from this page.

## Plan and usage

The workspace settings page shows your current plan tier (Starter, Pro, Premium, or Enterprise) and your real-time usage across the following limits:

| Limit | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Team members** | How many members are in your workspace vs your plan's seat limit |
| **Companies** | Number of company profiles created vs your plan's limit |
| **Active jobs** | Live job postings currently accepting applications |
| **CV Bank credits** | CVs uploaded to your talent bank |
| **Evaluation credits** | AI screening evaluations consumed |

Usage bars update in real time as your team works.

## Company profiles

Company profiles — including logo, name, industry, size, website, and location — are managed on the [Companies](/core-features/companies) page, not in workspace settings.

## Culture pages

Each company's culture page is configured from the **Culture** link on the company card in the Companies page. See the [Companies guide](/core-features/companies) for details.

## Danger zone

The danger zone contains irreversible actions like deleting the workspace. These require owner-level confirmation.

<Warning>
  Deleting a workspace permanently removes all jobs, candidates, settings, and data. This cannot be undone.
</Warning>
